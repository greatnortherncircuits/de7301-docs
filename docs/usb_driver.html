USB Driver Installation¶

The USB-C port may require drivers to be installed depending on your operating system

Mac¶

Drivers are distributed by Apple and no additional steps are required, it just works.

Windows¶

On Windows, a specific process is required to install drivers for the DE7301.

  1. Download and install Zadig, a utility to install custom drivers

  2. Open Zadig.exee, clicking “Yes” to any Windows prompts

  3. In the Options Menu, Check “List All Devices”

  4. In the Options menu, Uncheck “Ignore Hubs or Composite Parents”

  5. In the drop down menu locate “Dual RS232-HS (Composite Parent)

    • Do NOT select “Dual RS232-HS (Interface 0)”

    • Do NOT select “Dual RS232-HS (Interface 1)”.

  6. Verify that The USB ID matches the below picture.

  7. Ensure “WinUSB” is selected above the “Replace Driver” button

  8. After double checking the Device USB ID, click “Replace Driver”

  9. If you are prompted to confirm replacement of a system driver, click “Yes”

  10. Wait for installation to complete. This may take up to 5 minutes
